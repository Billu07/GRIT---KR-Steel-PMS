The app's current main feature is providing maintenance reports on equipments. The report generation feature needs support for the following options for showing and exporting to excel/word.

1. Filter by duration: Any one option at a time
   a. From Date and To Date : Records from say 09-Nov-23 to say 10-Nov-24
   b. Select Date : Records of a selected day
   c. Select Month : Records of a selected month
   d. Select Year : Records of a selected year

2. Filter by equipments : One or more equipments can be selected from the list of all equipments

Equipments and maintenance

Each equipment has a list of checkpoints or schedule of standard maintenance. A checkpoint can be either Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Every 3 Months, 6 Months or Yearly.

The maintenance are of 3 types
a) Predictive maintenance
b) Scheduled maintenance
c) Corrective maintenance

Predictive and corrective maintenance do not follow any predefined periods.

Scheduled maintenance follow the checkpoints defined for an equipment and they are done at the dates that follow the frequency of the checkpoint.
