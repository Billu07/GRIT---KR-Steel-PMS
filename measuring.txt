(Measurement Equipment)

1. LUX LEVEL METER
Daily
* Clean sensor and check physical condition
* Verify display and battery status
* Basic functional check
Weekly
* Inspect probe/cable (if applicable)
* Verify stable reading
Monthly
* Accuracy check with reference source
* Verify calibration due date
* Record inspection
Quarterly 
* Cross-check reading with calibrated reference instrument
* Inspect internal condition (battery terminals, connectors)
* Review usage log
Annually 
* Full calibration by accredited laboratory
* Obtain calibration certificate
* Update calibration label and record

* Verify alarm functions
Weekly
* Clean sensor inlet and check filter
* Inspect accessories
Monthly
* Full calibration with certified gas

Quarterly
* Sensor performance evaluation
* Replace filters if required
* Pump flow check (if pump type)
Annually 
* Full calibration by authorized service provider
* Sensor replacement if life expired
* Firmware update (if applicable)
* Maintain certification record
